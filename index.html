FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About
Psychotherapy in Florida

Everything you need to know before starting therapy with Serene Minds Psychotherapy.

Psychotherapy in Florida is licensed talk therapy delivered by a state-licensed mental health professional. Serene Minds Psychotherapy provides this care entirely through secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth, so you can attend sessions from anywhere in Florida. Fram Sarkari, M.S., LMHC offers individual therapy, couples counseling, family therapy, teen and child therapy, women's programs, and clinical supervision. Call (786) 972-7110 to get started.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy offers individual therapy, couples counseling, family therapy, teen and child therapy, women's programs (single parenting, divorce, and self-esteem support), online telehealth sessions, and clinical supervision for Registered Mental Health Counselor Interns. All services are available via secure telehealth throughout Florida. Browse the full list of therapy services or call (786) 972-7110 for a free 15-minute consultation.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy is open Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern Time, and is closed on Sundays. Saturday appointments are available throughout the day, which makes weekend therapy a regular option for clients with weekday work schedules. Call (786) 972-7110 or email info@serenemindspsychotherapy.com to find a time that fits your schedule.

Sessions are $150 each for 50-minute individual, couples, or family therapy. Serene Minds Psychotherapy is a private-pay practice and does not bill insurance; superbills are available so you can submit for out-of-network reimbursement on your own plan. See full details on the pricing page or call (786) 972-7110.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy treats anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, relationship issues, life transitions, self-esteem, ADHD, PTSD, OCD, bipolar disorder, postpartum depression, anger management, and behavioral concerns in children and teens. Treatment is tailored to each client using evidence-based approaches including CBT, DBT, mindfulness, and motivational interviewing. Call (786) 972-7110 to discuss your specific needs.

Because every session is delivered via telehealth, Serene Minds Psychotherapy serves clients in all 67 Florida counties — including Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Tampa, Orlando, Jacksonville, Hialeah, Pembroke Pines, Coral Springs, St. Petersburg, and Tallahassee. You can attend therapy from anywhere in Florida with a private space and a stable internet connection. Call (786) 972-7110 to get started.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy provides therapy in English, Gujarati, and Hindi. If you or a family member are more comfortable in Gujarati or Hindi, sessions can be conducted entirely in that language. Call (786) 972-7110 or email info@serenemindspsychotherapy.com to request a session in your preferred language.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy accepts Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and Zelle. HSA and FSA cards are also accepted for eligible expenses. Payment is due at the time of service, and superbills are provided on request so you can submit to your insurance for out-of-network reimbursement.

Fram Sarkari, M.S., LMHC has over 20 years of experience in mental health care and has lived and practiced in Florida for more than 20 years. He is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor and a Qualified Clinical Supervisor for Registered Mental Health Counselor Interns. Learn more on the about page.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy combines 20+ years of clinical experience with culturally sensitive care in English, Gujarati, and Hindi — a combination that is rare among Florida telehealth practices. Every session is delivered one-on-one by Fram Sarkari, M.S., LMHC (no rotating clinicians or app intermediaries), and the practice offers extended evening and Saturday hours from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Call (786) 972-7110 to schedule a free 15-minute consultation.

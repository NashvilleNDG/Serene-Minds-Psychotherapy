How Does Payment Work?

Serene Minds Psychotherapy is a private-pay practice and does not accept insurance; each session is $150, charged at the time of service. We accept major credit and debit cards, Zelle, HSA, and FSA, and provide superbills on request for out-of-network reimbursement.

What Are the Benefits of Private Pay?

✓

Complete Privacy

Your records stay between us. No insurance company has access to your treatment details.

✓

Flexible Treatment

Treatment length and approach based on your needs, not insurance limitations.

✓

No Diagnosis Required

Insurance requires a mental health diagnosis. Private pay offers more flexibility.

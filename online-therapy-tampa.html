How Online Therapy Works at Serene Minds

A device with a camera and microphone — smartphone, tablet, or laptop — a reliable internet connection, and a private space for 50 minutes. Tampa's broadband infrastructure is excellent — standard home WiFi is more than sufficient. You receive a secure link by email before each session — click it and you are connected. No downloads, no accounts, no technical setup required.

All sessions are conducted via a fully encrypted, HIPAA-compliant video platform. Your sessions are private, your records are secure, and no third party has access to your clinical information — from the technology to the billing.

As a private-pay practice, no insurance company has access to your diagnosis, records, or treatment notes. In Tampa's growing professional networks — where reputation and discretion matter — the confidentiality of private-pay online therapy is a genuine clinical and practical advantage.

Monday through Saturday, 8am to 8pm — scheduled via Calendly at any time with immediate confirmation. Evening and Saturday sessions for Tampa professionals whose working day makes daytime appointments difficult. Whether you attend during a lunch break in Channelside, after the school run in Westchase, or from your home in New Tampa — the schedule works around Tampa life.

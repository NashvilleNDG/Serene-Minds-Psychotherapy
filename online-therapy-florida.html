What Is Online Therapy?

Online therapy — also called telehealth therapy or virtual therapy — is professional psychotherapy conducted via secure video rather than in person. It is the same evidence-based treatment you would receive in a traditional office setting, delivered through a platform designed to protect your privacy and meet HIPAA requirements.

At Serene Minds Psychotherapy, all online therapy sessions are conducted via a secure, fully encrypted video platform. Sessions are 50 minutes long and available by appointment Monday through Saturday, 8am to 8pm. All you need is a private space, a reliable internet connection, and a device with a camera.

Research consistently confirms that online therapy produces outcomes equivalent to in-person therapy across a wide range of mental health conditions — including depression, anxiety, trauma, relationship issues, and more. For many clients, telehealth is not just convenient — it is their preferred format.

“Online therapy removes every barrier between you and the support you deserve — distance, traffic, scheduling, and stigma. All that remains is the work.”

How Online Therapy at Serene Minds Works

Reach out by phone at (786) 972-7110, email info@serenemindspsychotherapy.com, or the contact form. Fram will schedule a free 15-minute consultation — phone or video — to share what you are looking for and confirm fit. No obligation before that conversation.

All sessions use a fully encrypted, HIPAA-compliant video platform. You receive a secure link by email — click it, allow camera and microphone access, and begin. It works on a smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop with a camera. If you have technical trouble, Fram is available by phone.

Sessions are 50 minutes — the same as a traditional appointment. They are structured, focused, and goal-directed. Between sessions you may receive exercises, reflection prompts, or behavioral experiments — because change happens in daily life.

Your sessions are conducted on an encrypted, HIPAA-compliant platform. As private pay, Serene Minds does not submit insurance claims — so insurers do not access your diagnosis or records through billing. What you share stays between you and Fram.

Sessions are available Monday through Saturday, 8am to 8pm. Booking, rescheduling, and cancellations are managed through Calendly. There is no cancellation fee — Fram asks that you notify him as soon as possible so the time can be offered to others.

Sessions are $150 per 50 minutes, payable at time of service. Serene Minds is private pay and does not accept insurance — supporting confidentiality and treatment guided by your needs. Major credit and debit cards and Zelle are accepted. HSA and FSA accounts are accepted. A detailed superbill can be submitted to your insurer for possible out-of-network reimbursement — many clients recover 50–80% depending on the plan.

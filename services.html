METHODOLOGY

What Treatment Approaches Does Serene Minds Use?

Every client is unique, so Fram Sarkari, M.S., LMHC draws from multiple evidence-based approaches — CBT, DBT, mindfulness, solution-focused therapy, motivational interviewing, and REBT — to create a personalized treatment plan that works for you.

Motivational Interviewing

A collaborative approach that strengthens your own motivation for change.

Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT)

Identify and change negative thought patterns that affect behavior and emotions.

Mindfulness & Meditation

Develop present-moment awareness to reduce stress and increase well-being.

Solution-Focused Therapy

Goal-oriented approach focusing on solutions rather than problems.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Build skills in mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness.

REBT

Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy to challenge irrational beliefs.

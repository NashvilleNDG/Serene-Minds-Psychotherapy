ABOUT ME

Who Is the Psychotherapist
at Serene Minds?

Faramroze (Fram) Sarkari, M.S., LMHC is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor and Qualified Clinical Supervisor offering psychotherapy in Florida via secure telehealth. As the founder of Serene Minds Psychotherapy, he has 20+ years of clinical experience helping individuals, families, and communities navigate their mental health journeys with compassion and evidence-based care.

Born and raised in India, Fram has lived and practiced in Florida for more than 20 years. He is fluent in English, Gujarati, and Hindi, allowing him to provide culturally sensitive therapy to clients from diverse backgrounds throughout the state — from Miami to Tampa to Jacksonville and beyond.

His passion for understanding the human mind drives his ongoing pursuit of knowledge. He is currently researching the effects of schizophrenia symptoms on suicidal ideation and plans to pursue a doctorate in Neuropsychology. Outside the therapy room, Fram offers clinical supervision for Registered Mental Health Counselor Interns working toward Florida LMHC licensure.

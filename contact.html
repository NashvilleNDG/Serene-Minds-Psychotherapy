Contacting Serene Minds

How to reach Serene Minds Psychotherapy, what to expect, and how to book.

You can contact Serene Minds Psychotherapy by phone or text at (786) 972-7110, by email at info@serenemindspsychotherapy.com, or by using the contact form on this page. You can also book directly through the online scheduler. Office hours are Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy is open Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern Time, and closed on Sundays. Saturday and evening appointments are available, which makes scheduling around work or school easier. Call (786) 972-7110 to find a time that fits.

Messages sent through the contact form or by email are typically answered within 24 hours. If you need a faster response, call or text (786) 972-7110 directly during office hours, Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Yes. Serene Minds Psychotherapy offers a free 15-minute consultation so you can ask questions, describe what you're looking for, and decide whether it's a good fit before booking a full session. Call (786) 972-7110 or use the contact form to request one.

Serene Minds Psychotherapy is a telehealth-only practice based in Florida — there is no in-person office. Every session is delivered via secure, HIPAA-compliant video, so you can attend from anywhere in Florida, including Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Tampa, Orlando, and Jacksonville.

Because all sessions are online, Serene Minds Psychotherapy serves clients throughout the entire state of Florida — including Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Tampa, Orlando, Jacksonville, Hialeah, Pembroke Pines, and Coral Springs. Call (786) 972-7110 to get started from anywhere in Florida.

Fram Sarkari, M.S., LMHC provides therapy in English, Gujarati, and Hindi. If you or a family member are more comfortable in Gujarati or Hindi, the entire session can be conducted in that language. Mention your preference when you call (786) 972-7110 or email info@serenemindspsychotherapy.com.
